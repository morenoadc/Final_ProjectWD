About DM Flooring Services

DM Flooring Services is a family owned business that focuses on quality work, clear communication, and respectful service in every home we enter.

Our Story

We started DM Flooring Services after years of hands–on experience working on residential and small commercial projects. What began as a few jobs for friends and family has grown into a trusted local company serving Cincinnati and the surrounding areas.

Our goal is simple: floors that look beautiful and last, installed by people you feel comfortable having in your home.

What We Care About

Quality

We pay attention to subfloor prep, layout, and finishing so your floors look good up close and from across the room.

Respect

We show up on time, protect your space while we work, and clean up when the job is done.

Honesty

You’ll get clear prices, realistic timelines, and honest suggestions about what will work best in your home.

How Our Process Works

  1. Free estimate: We visit your space, talk about options, and provide a quote.
  2. Scheduling: We set a start date and explain how long the job will take and what to expect.
  3. Installation / refinishing: We complete the work, keeping you updated along the way.
  4. Final walk–through: We check the details with you and make sure you’re happy with the results.
